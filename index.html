Barcodes All in 1

QR Scanner & Barcodes All in 1 is a barcode management app that capable of scanning 14 types of barcodes including QR Code, PDF417, Data Matrix, Aztec, EAN 13, EAN 8, UPC A, UPC E, CODE 128, Code 93, Code 39, CODABAR, ISBN, ITF via your device Camera. You can also generate your own barcodes by creating your own database, import/export as Excel CSV file and share them easily. QR & Barcodes All in 1 is periodically being updated for enhancing functionalities of the app to provide a great user experience for you.
